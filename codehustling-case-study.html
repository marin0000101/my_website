CodeHustling Case Study

Client Overview

CodeHustling is a website created for an Instagram community led by Fabion Kallaku, who posts about web development and the web industry. With over 185k followers, his community is highly engaged and active. The purpose of the website was to provide a central hub with all the information about Fabion and his community.

Project Goals

Fabion wanted to achieve a centralized, informative website that provided comprehensive details about him and his Instagram community. The goal was to have all information in one place to enhance accessibility and engagement.

Process

  1. 1. Discovery Call: Initial consultation to understand goals and expectations
  2. 2. Market Analysis: Researched the gaming and Web3 industries, and analyzed competitors

  4. 4. Development Phase: Built the WordPress site, optimized for blogging and updates
  5. 5. SEO Optimization: Boosted website visibility with SEO strategies
  6. 6. Hosting and Domain Setup: Managed hosting and domain for full functionality
  7. 7. Ongoing Maintenance: Provided monthly maintenance for smooth operation

Results

The website effectively centralized all information about Fabion and his community, making it easier for followers to access relevant content. This enhanced the credibility and visibility of his brand, leading to higher engagement and a more organized presentation of information.
