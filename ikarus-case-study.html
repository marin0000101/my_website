Ikarus Agency Case Study

Client Overview

Ikarus Agency was a professional service provider specializing in web development, design, and digital marketing. The website showcased their portfolio, services, and contact information, offering insights into their expertise and previous projects. During their operation, they worked with more than 15 clients. Eventually, the team transitioned to a new business venture, ceasing operations under the Ikarus Agency name.

Project Goals

The goal was to create a comprehensive and visually appealing website that effectively presented the agency's services, portfolio, and contact information to attract potential clients and establish credibility in the digital space.
